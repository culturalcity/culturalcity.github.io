# 閱大安 圖卡生成規範（AI 圖像模型）

用於 **Gemini Nano Banana / ChatGPT Image / 其他 AI 圖像模型** 產生 LINE 群組公告圖卡。
網站本身有自己的 CSS 系統，請見 [`VI.md`](./VI.md)，本文不涵蓋。

---

## 為什麼需要這份規範

LINE 是視覺平台、公告以圖為主（住戶滑時不會錯過）。AI 圖像模型可快速產生背景與構圖，但若不給品牌指引，每次出圖風格不一致，閱大安的視覺記憶會散掉。本規範把 [`VI.md`](./VI.md) 翻譯成 AI 模型聽得懂的語言。

---

## 核心風格 baseline

**這段直接貼進每次 prompt 開頭：**

```
Visual style: refined minimal Japanese editorial aesthetic, muji and mingei craft sensibility.
Muted earth tone palette: warm beige, soft cream, deep brown, charcoal. NO bright saturated colors.
Low contrast, soft natural diffused lighting, subtle texture (linen, washi paper, fine stone).
Composition: clean, generous negative space, asymmetric balance, no rule-of-thirds clichés.
Mood: quiet, considerate, residential, neighborly — not corporate, not commercial, not dramatic.
```

## 色票（給 AI 的版本）

VI 色票翻譯成自然語言，給模型參考：

| VI 變數 | HEX | 描述詞（給 prompt 用） |
|---|---|---|
| `--wg1`  | `#EAE7E1` | warm beige cream background |
| `--white`| `#F7F5F2` | soft off-white paper tone |
| `--wg41` | `#3C3835` | deep warm charcoal brown |
| `--wg9`  | `#898480` | muted taupe gray |
| `--dp`   | `#1F5C38` | deep forest green（限正向） |
| `--dn`   | `#8C1F1F` | brick red oxide（限警示） |
| `--warn` | `#8C5A00` | warm amber brown（高溫類） |

**禁用色域**：飽和原色（pure red/blue/yellow）、霓虹色、純黑、純白、彩虹漸層。

## 構圖與規格

- **長寬比**：方形 1:1（LINE feed 預覽不裁切）或直幅 4:5（多文字資訊時）
- **解析度**：1080×1080 或 1080×1350，LINE 上限以內
- **留白**：四周至少 8% 留白，文字／主視覺別貼邊
- **頂部視覺帶（選用）**：可在最上方加一條深褐色帶（`#3C3835`），帶上低對比織紋——呼應網站 header，建立視覺連續性
- **文字位置**：預留下方 30-40% 給後製文字（見下節）

## ⚠️ 文字一律後製，不靠 AI 寫

**所有 AI 圖像模型寫 CJK 中文都不準**（會出現像中文但不是中文的字、筆畫錯亂、簡繁混雜）。處理方式：

1. **Prompt 裡明確說 "no text, no letters, no characters anywhere in the image"**
2. 生圖時只生**背景／插畫／視覺主體**
3. 文字（標題、內文、日期）用 **Canva / Figma / Photopea** 後製疊上

如果模型仍偷塞中文進來，重生或在 Photopea 用 clone 工具塗掉。

## 常見公告類型 prompt 模板

每個模板都已內建上方「核心風格 baseline」，直接複製可用。生成後在後製工具加文字。

### A. 高溫／黃燈／天氣警示類

```
A minimal editorial illustration for a residential community heat advisory.
Visual style: refined minimal Japanese editorial aesthetic, muji and mingei craft sensibility.
Muted earth tone palette: warm beige background (#EAE7E1), soft amber brown accent (#8C5A00),
deep charcoal brown details (#3C3835). NO bright colors, NO red, NO orange.
Subject: a simple stylized sun motif or warm wind pattern, drawn in single-line brush style,
positioned in the upper third. Soft watercolor or washi paper texture background.
Lower 40% of canvas: empty soft beige space (reserved for text overlay later).
NO text, NO letters, NO characters anywhere in the image.
Aspect ratio 1:1, 1080x1080, soft natural diffused lighting, gentle and reassuring mood.
```

### B. 施工／工程／設備類

```
A minimal editorial illustration for a residential community construction notice.
Visual style: refined minimal Japanese editorial aesthetic, muji and mingei craft sensibility.
Muted earth tone palette: warm beige background (#EAE7E1), deep charcoal brown (#3C3835),
muted taupe gray accents (#898480). Restrained, no decorative flourishes.
Subject: a single isolated tool, wrench, or geometric construction element drawn in
minimal line-art style, centered in the upper half. Soft linen texture background.
Lower 40% of canvas: empty soft beige space (reserved for text overlay later).
NO text, NO letters, NO characters, NO Chinese text anywhere in the image.
Aspect ratio 1:1, 1080x1080, soft natural lighting, calm functional mood.
```

### C. 活動／聚會／節慶類

```
A minimal editorial illustration for a residential community gathering.
Visual style: refined minimal Japanese editorial aesthetic, muji and mingei craft sensibility,
slightly warmer and more inviting than usual.
Muted earth tone palette: warm beige and cream background (#EAE7E1, #F7F5F2),
soft deep forest green accent (#1F5C38), warm charcoal details (#3C3835).
Subject: a simple still-life arrangement (a small ceramic vessel, a single branch, soft fabric)
in the upper two-thirds, brushstroke illustration style. Washi paper texture background.
Lower 30% of canvas: empty soft cream space (reserved for text overlay later).
NO text, NO letters, NO characters anywhere in the image.
Aspect ratio 1:1, 1080x1080, soft natural window light, warm and neighborly mood.
```

### D. 規約／重申／文件類

```
A minimal editorial header image for a residential community policy document.
Visual style: refined minimal Japanese editorial aesthetic, muji and mingei craft sensibility,
document-like and restrained.
Muted earth tone palette: soft off-white paper background (#F7F5F2),
deep warm charcoal brown lines (#3C3835), no accent colors.
Subject: clean geometric pattern, single horizontal rule, or fine linen-weave texture.
Composition is symmetric and quiet. NO illustration of people, NO objects, NO icons.
Lower 50% of canvas: empty soft off-white space (reserved for text overlay later).
NO text, NO letters, NO characters anywhere in the image.
Aspect ratio 4:5, 1080x1350, soft even lighting, formal and dignified mood.
```

## 負面 prompt（建議加在每次生成）

如果模型支援 negative prompt 欄位，貼這段：

```
no bright saturated colors, no neon, no rainbow gradients, no pure red, no pure blue,
no cartoon characters, no anime style, no 3d render, no photorealistic people,
no Western architecture, no skyscrapers, no busy backgrounds, no stock photo aesthetic,
no text, no letters, no Chinese characters, no logos, no watermarks,
no harsh shadows, no high contrast, no dramatic lighting.
```

## 工作流程建議

1. **生成背景／主視覺**（AI 模型）—— 用上方模板
2. **若文字混入**，Photopea 或 Canva 塗掉
3. **後製疊文字**（Canva 推薦）：
   - 字型：思源黑體 / Noto Sans TC（網站同款）
   - 主標 36-48px、副標 18-22px、內文 14-16px
   - 文字顏色用 `#3C3835`（不要純黑）
   - 留白比例 ≥ 30%
4. **匯出 PNG** 上傳 LINE，搭配下方文字模板（含網站連結）

## 與 VI 的對應關係

| VI 元素 | 圖卡上的對應 |
|---|---|
| `global.css :root` 色票 | 上方「色票」表的 HEX 與描述詞 |
| `Noto Sans TC` 字型 | 後製文字一律用 Noto Sans TC |
| Header 紋理（雙層 gradient on `#3C3835`） | 圖卡頂部視覺帶（選用） |
| 整體 muji / 民藝感 | 核心風格 baseline 的形容詞集合 |

VI 異動時記得同步檢視本文，避免圖卡與網站視覺脫節。

---

**最後提醒**：AI 圖卡是為了**省時間**，不是為了「比手繪好看」。如果某個公告 30 秒口頭講清楚，不用做圖卡——文字版就好。圖卡的成本上限：**生成 + 後製不超過 10 分鐘**。超過代表 prompt 沒收斂或內容本身不適合圖像化，回頭改純文字。
