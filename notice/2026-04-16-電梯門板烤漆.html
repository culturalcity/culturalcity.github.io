電梯門板刮傷修復烤漆施工通知 Notice: Elevator Door Panel Scratch Repair and Repainting エレベーター扉パネル傷修復・焼付塗装施工のご案内

工程通知 2026/04/16 閱大安管理中心
說明

由於社區部分樓層電梯門板有刮傷情形，為維護公共設施之美觀與質感，由建商安排專業廠商進行門板修繕及烤漆作業，敬請各位住戶配合。

施工資訊
施工日期115年04月16日（四）至04月18日（六）
施工時間每日上午9時至下午5時
施工範圍 以下樓層之電梯門板
3F 5F 8F 9F 11F 12F 13F 14F
影響說明
  • 施工區域及電梯車廂內可能會有強烈漆味，建議對氣味敏感或患有呼吸道疾病的住戶，於施工期間避開該電梯或配戴口罩。
  • 現場將加強通風，並採取適當換氣措施，縮短異味留存時間。
施工期間管理中心將全力督促廠商加快進度，並注意現場安全維護。造成不便，敬請見諒，感謝您的體諒與配合！
閱大安管理中心・如有疑問請洽管理中心
Notice

Scratches have been found on elevator door panels on several floors of our community. To maintain the appearance and quality of our shared facilities, the developer has arranged for a professional contractor to carry out panel repair and repainting work.

Work Details
DatesApril 16 (Thu) – April 18 (Sat), 2026
Hours9:00 AM – 5:00 PM daily
Scope Elevator door panels on the following floors
3F 5F 8F 9F 11F 12F 13F 14F
Impact
  • There may be a strong paint odour in the work areas and elevator cabins. Residents who are sensitive to odours or have respiratory conditions are advised to avoid the elevator or wear a mask during the work period.
  • Ventilation will be enhanced on-site to minimise the duration of any odour.
The Management Center will closely supervise the contractor to ensure efficient progress and on-site safety throughout the work period. We sincerely apologise for any inconvenience and thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
