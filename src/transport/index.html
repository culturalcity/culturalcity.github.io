GREEN TRANSPORT

綠色交通資訊專區Green Transport Guideグリーン交通ガイド

CULTURAL CITY COMMUNITY · 閱大安管理委員會

本社區位於臺北市大安區羅斯福路二段85號，緊鄰古亭捷運站，搭乘大眾運輸與騎乘自行車皆十分便利。離開社區時，邀請住戶一起優先選擇低碳的移動方式，響應淨零新生活、減少碳排放。

🚇捷運 MRT
古亭站 3 號出口，步行約 2 分鐘
雙線轉乘站：綠線（松山新店線）往新店／往松山、橘線（中和新蘆線）往南勢角／往蘆洲・迴龍
古亭站時刻表與首末班車 列車即時到站動態

捷運古亭站(羅斯福) 捷運古亭站(和平) 羅斯福金門街口

Our community is located at No. 85, Sec. 2, Roosevelt Rd., Da'an District, Taipei, right beside Guting MRT Station, with excellent access to public transport and cycling. When you head out, we invite every resident to choose low-carbon travel, supporting net-zero living and cutting carbon emissions.

🚇MRT
Guting Station, Exit 3 — about a 2-minute walk
Interchange of two lines: Green Line (Songshan–Xindian) toward Xindian / Songshan, and Orange Line (Zhonghe–Xinlu) toward Nanshijiao / Luzhou·Huilong.
