說明

本社區地下四樓（B4F）揚水 B 號機之雙拉桿式防震軟管發生故障，將由太古華電進行更新施工。施工期間需停水，謹此通知，並請住戶預作準備、配合相關事項。

施工資訊
日期115 年 6 月 10 日（星期三）
時間上午 09:30 起 ~ 下午 17:00 止
項目B4F 揚水 B 號機雙拉桿式防震軟管故障更新
影響施工期間停水
敬請住戶配合
  • 請於施工前儲水備用。
  • 施工期間請勿至地下室開啟揚水泵浦控制手動開關，以免馬達空轉損壞。
  • 家中裝有加壓馬達、淨水設備等與供水相關設備者，請於當日先行關閉，復水後再行開啟，以防損壞。
施工造成不便之處，敬請見諒。施工單位為太古華電實業（工務管理部）。如有疑問，歡迎洽管理中心。
閱大安管理委員會・如有疑問請洽管理中心
Background

The dual-rod anti-vibration flexible hose on Booster Pump No. B, located on the fourth basement level (B4F), has failed and will be replaced by Taikwu Industry Co. The water supply will be suspended during the work. Please prepare in advance and observe the requests below.

Work Details
DateWednesday, June 10, 2026
Time09:30 – 17:00
ScopeReplacement of the faulty dual-rod anti-vibration flexible hose on Booster Pump No. B (B4F)
ImpactWater supply suspended during the work
What Residents Are Asked to Do
  • Please store water in advance.
  • During the work, do not operate the booster pump manual control switch in the basement, to prevent dry-running damage to the motor.
  • If you have a pressure-boosting pump, water purifier or other equipment connected to the water supply, please switch it off for the day and turn it back on after the supply resumes, to avoid damage.
We apologize for any inconvenience. Work is carried out by Taikwu Industry Co. (Engineering Dept.). For any questions, please contact the Management Center.
Cultural City Management Committee · For questions, please contact the Management Center
