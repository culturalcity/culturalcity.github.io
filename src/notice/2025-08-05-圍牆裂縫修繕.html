Background

Minor cracks appeared in the rear perimeter wall of the community following last year's earthquake. To ensure the safety of all residents and maintain the structural integrity of the building, the Management Committee has engaged a professional contractor to carry out the necessary repair works.

Work Schedule
DateMonday, 11 August 2025
Hours08:30–17:30
LocationRear perimeter wall
☔ If rain is forecast, work will be postponed. A further notice will be issued accordingly.
Resident Requests
  • Please keep clear of the work area to ensure personal safety.
  • Some noise and inconvenience may occur during the works. We sincerely apologize for any disruption.
  • If you have any questions, please contact the Management Center.
Thank you for your cooperation and understanding.

昨年の地震により、本マンション後方の外周壁に小さな亀裂が生じました。居住者の皆様の安全と建物の構造的健全性を確保するため、管理委員会は専門業者に修繕工事を依頼いたしました。
