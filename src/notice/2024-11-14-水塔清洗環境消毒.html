說明
  1. 社區實施定期水塔清洗及環境消毒。
  2. 施工日期：113年11月18日（週一）。施作時間：水塔清洗 09:00–17:00；環境消毒 13:00–15:00。
  3. 水塔清洗前請居家先行儲水；13–15F 高樓層住戶請先將加壓馬達關閉，以免造成設備損壞。
  4. 消毒作業區域：地下室停車空間 B1–B4、1F 前庭及後庭、頂樓 RF 室外空間、A・B 逃生梯。消毒期間請勿在上述空間逗留，以免造成身體不適。
施工期間造成不便，敬請見諒。
Details
  1. The community will carry out scheduled water tank cleaning and environmental disinfection.
  2. Date: Monday, November 18, 2024. Times: water tank cleaning 09:00–17:00; disinfection 13:00–15:00.
  3. Please store water in advance. Residents on floors 13–15 should switch off their water pressure pumps to prevent equipment damage.
  4. Disinfection areas: basement parking levels B1–B4, the 1F front and back gardens, the RF outdoor area, and emergency staircases A and B. Please do not linger in these areas during disinfection to avoid discomfort.
We apologize for any inconvenience during the work.
