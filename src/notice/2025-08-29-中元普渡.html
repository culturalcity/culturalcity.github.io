About This Event

The Ghost Festival (中元節, Zhongyuanjie) is a traditional Taiwanese folk ritual observed during the seventh lunar month. On the designated day, communities hold a communal offering ceremony (普渡, Pǔdù) to pay respects to ancestral spirits and wandering souls. The Management Committee is pleased to organize this annual event on behalf of all residents.

Event Details
Date & TimeSaturday, 20 September 2025 (Lunar 7th Month, 29th Day)　14:00
VenueIn front of the main lobby entrance
Notes
  • Offerings: The Management Center will provide offering tables, ritual items, spirit money, and basic offerings. Residents who wish to participate may also bring their own offerings such as three sacrificial meats, fruit, biscuits, beverages, incense sticks and candles (according to personal customs and as you see fit), and place them on the offering table.
  • Setup time: Please arrange your offerings neatly on the table by 14:00, following staff guidance. All personal offerings should be retrieved after the ceremony.
  • Spirit money burning: In the interest of safety and environmental regulations, no incinerator will be provided on-site. Spirit money will be collected centrally and sent to the Environmental Protection Bureau for official burning. Residents are also asked not to burn offerings on their balconies or in any common areas.
行事について

中元節（ちゅうげんせつ）は、旧暦7月に行われる台湾の伝統的な民間行事です。この時期に行われる「普渡（プードゥ）」は、先祖の霊や浮遊する魂に供物を捧げ、供養する儀式です。管理委員会は毎年、全居住者様のためにこの地域行事を執り行っております。
