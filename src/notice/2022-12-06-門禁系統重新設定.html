說明
  1. 今日門禁系統異常，導致門禁卡、E-TAG 無法正常使用，部分門禁卡及 E-TAG 車輛需重新設定。
  2. 111年9月7日後交屋的屋主，請撥空至管理中心重新設定包裹領取及 E-TAG 車輛的進出管制。
  3. 設定時間為上午 7 點至晚上 6 點，由早班禮賓及總幹事代為設定。
造成不便之處敬請見諒。
Details
  1. Due to an access control system error today, some access cards and E-TAGs are not working properly and need to be reset.
  2. Owners who took possession after September 7, 2022: please visit the Management Center to reset your parcel pickup registration and E-TAG vehicle access.
  3. Resets can be done between 7:00 AM and 6:00 PM, assisted by the morning concierge and the property manager.
We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

  3. 設定時間は午前7時〜午後6時で、早番コンシェルジュと管理員が対応いたします。
