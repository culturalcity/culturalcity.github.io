說明

施工時間：112年3月13日（一）上午 9 點至下午 5 點

  1. 機車位加裝不銹鋼保護板施工。

造成不便之處敬請見諒，謝謝。
Details

Work period: Monday, March 13, 2023, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

  1. Stainless-steel protective panels will be installed at the scooter parking spaces.
  2. During the work, please temporarily move your scooter to the 1F accessible parking space, and return it to your original space once the work is complete.
We apologize for any inconvenience caused. Thank you.
