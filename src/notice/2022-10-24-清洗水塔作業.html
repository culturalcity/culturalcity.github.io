說明
  1. 本次社區清洗水塔作業時間改為 10/28（五）及 10/29（六），共兩天。

Details
  1. Water tank cleaning has been rescheduled to two days: Friday, October 28 and Saturday, October 29.
  2. Fri, Oct 28, 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM: cleaning of the basement water tanks (B1–B4). As the rooftop tank holds limited water, supply interruptions are possible — please store water in advance. Normal supply is expected to resume by 5:00 PM.
  3. Sat, Oct 29, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM: cleaning of the rooftop water tank (R3F). Water will be shut off during cleaning — please store water in advance. Normal supply is expected to resume by 8:00 PM.
